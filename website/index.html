Our Philosophy

In the early days of the internet, people made software because they wanted to help others. No subscription traps, no ad-surveillance, no dark patterns — just useful tools shared freely.

We're bringing that spirit back. Every app in this collection is completely free, contains no ads, collects no data, and requires no internet connection to use. They exist to be genuinely useful, nothing more.

These apps will always be free. If you find them useful and want to help cover the cost of keeping them available, donations are welcome but never expected.
