Privacy Policy

Forever Free Apps — Last updated: March 31, 2026

Forever Free Apps is a collection of free, ad-free utilities built in the spirit of early internet shareware. This privacy policy applies to all apps published under the Forever Free Apps name on Google Play and the Apple App Store.

The Short Version

We collect nothing. Zero. None of your data ever leaves your device. No accounts, no tracking, no ads, no analytics. These apps work fully offline and have no reason to phone home.

Our Apps

Available on Google Play

NATO Alphabet Trainer

A reference and quiz app for learning the NATO phonetic alphabet. This app does not collect any data whatsoever.

  • No internet connection required or used
  • No analytics or crash reporting
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No account or login required
  • No permissions requested

Data Collection

None of our apps collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not use analytics tools (no Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • We do not serve ads (no AdMob, no third-party ad networks)
  • We do not use crash reporting services
  • We do not track how you use the app
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location
  • We do not store anything on external servers

Permissions

Our apps request no special system permissions. They run entirely within their sandbox.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use no third-party SDKs, services, or APIs that collect data. The only external link in any of our apps is a voluntary donation link to Ko-fi, which only activates if you tap it.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data from anyone, our apps are safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date. We do not anticipate meaningful changes since we don't collect data.
